## 1. Executive Summary

**Project Name:** Maya Kingdom Two  
**Type:** ACTION / STRATEGY  
**Platform Target:** PC (Steam), Mobile (iOS/Android)  
**Target Release:** Q4 2027  
**Team:** NorthernStar Studio

**One-Liner:** "Build your dynasty in a living Mesoamerican world where every choice shapes your civilization's fate."

**Summary:**  
A historical action-strategy game set in a vibrant Mesoamerican world. Players build and manage their Maya kingdom, making strategic decisions about resource management, warfare, diplomacy, and religion. Building on the success of Maya Kingdom, this sequel expands the gameplay with deeper city-building, new civilizations (Aztec, Inca), and a dynamic campaign mode.

## 2. Market Opportunity

### 2.1 Target Audience

| Segment | Description | Priority |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| Primary | Strategy gamers, 18-40 | High |
| Secondary | History enthusiasts, 25-45 | Medium |
| Tertiary | Casual city-builders, 18-55 | Low |

### 2.2 Market Fit

- **Current trends:** Historical strategy games seeing resurgence (Civilization VII, Age of Empires). Indie historical games perform well on Steam (PTerodactyl, Old World).
- **Gap identified:** Few high-quality indie Maya/Aztec civilization games. Most are either AAA or low-quality.
- **Why now:** Growing interest in non-European history. Platform available (Steam, mobile). Success of Maya Kingdom provides foundation.

### 2.3 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Differentiation |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| Civilization VII | Brand recognition, deep mechanics | High price, complex | Focused Maya/Aztec narrative, accessible |
| Old World | Quality writing, replayability | Niche audience | Lower price point, mobile support |
| PTerodactyl games | Unique art direction | Limited scope | Better graphics, full campaign |

## 3. Value Proposition

### Core Hook
Build your Maya dynasty from humble beginnings to a powerful civilization, facing historical events, natural disasters, and rival factions.

### Key Features (MVP)
1. Campaign mode with historical scenarios
2. City-building with resource management
3. Turn-based tactical combat
4. Diplomatic system with AI neighbors
5. Religion and culture system

### Why Players Will Love It
- Unique historical setting not oversaturated
- Accessible but deep gameplay
- Beautiful art inspired by Mesoamerican culture
- Replayable with multiple civilizations
